Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 1, 2026  ·  Last Updated: June 9, 2026  ·  Applies to: Thaja Fruit Bowl, Mancherial, Telangana, India

Your privacy matters to us. Thaja Fruit Bowl collects only the information needed to deliver your orders and keep you informed. We do not sell your personal data to anyone.

01

Who We Are

Thaja Fruit Bowl ("we", "us", "our") is a fresh fruit bowl delivery service operating in Mancherial, Telangana, India. We deliver handcrafted fruit bowls made from 100% natural ingredients directly to your home.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information you provide when you place an order, contact us via WhatsApp, or visit our website.

◆ ◆ ◆
02

Information We Collect

We collect the following information only when you voluntarily provide it to us:

  • Full name — to address your order and delivery
  • Phone number (WhatsApp) — to confirm orders, share delivery updates, and send receipts
  • Delivery address — to deliver your fruit bowls to the correct location in Mancherial
  • Order details — the items, quantities, and special instructions you request
  • Subscription preferences — if you sign up for a daily or weekly delivery plan
  • Health or dietary preferences (optional) — only if you share them to help us suggest suitable bowls

We do not collect payment card numbers, government IDs, or any sensitive financial data. Cash or UPI payments are settled in person or via a third-party UPI app (e.g., Google Pay, PhonePe) whose own privacy policies apply.

◆ ◆ ◆
03

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Processing and fulfilling your fruit bowl orders
  • Confirming orders and communicating delivery status via WhatsApp
  • Managing daily or subscription delivery schedules
  • Sending you updates about new bowl offerings, seasonal specials, or price changes — only if you have opted in
  • Resolving complaints or questions about your order
  • Improving our service based on general feedback patterns

We will never use your information for unrelated marketing, spam, or automated profiling.

◆ ◆ ◆
04

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

We may share limited information in these specific circumstances:

  • Delivery personnel — your name and address are shared only with the delivery staff member assigned to your order, solely to complete delivery
  • Legal obligation — if required by Indian law, a court order, or government authority

No other sharing occurs. We do not use advertising networks, analytics platforms that track individuals, or data brokers.

◆ ◆ ◆
05

WhatsApp & Communications

We communicate primarily through WhatsApp. By sharing your phone number to place an order, you agree to receive order-related messages on WhatsApp from Thaja Fruit Bowl.

Promotional messages (e.g., new bowls, offers) are sent only to customers who have explicitly opted in. You can opt out at any time by replying "STOP" or messaging us directly.

WhatsApp's own privacy policy governs how Meta processes messages on its platform. We recommend reviewing it at whatsapp.com/legal/privacy-policy.

◆ ◆ ◆
06

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as you are an active customer or as required to resolve disputes, comply with legal obligations, or maintain order records.

  • Order records — kept for up to 3 years for accounting and dispute resolution
  • Inactive customers — contact details are deleted or anonymised after 12 months of inactivity, unless you ask us to remove them sooner
  • Opt-out requests — marketing records are deleted within 30 days of your opt-out

07

Your Rights

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India) and applicable consumer protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal data, subject to legal retention obligations
  • Withdraw consent — stop receiving promotional communications at any time
  • Grievance redressal — raise a complaint if you believe your data is misused

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in Section 10 below. We will respond within 15 business days.

◆ ◆ ◆
08

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. Customer data is stored in access-restricted systems and shared with staff only on a need-to-know basis.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. If you believe your information has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

09

Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for adults placing orders on behalf of their household. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 18 without parental consent. If you believe a child has provided us information without consent, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
